Experiment 1: Preaparation of Urea-Formaldehyde resin

Aim: Preparation of Urea-Formaldehyde resin

Step 1: Collect All the materials requires for the experiment.

Audio explanation:

APPARATUS : Beaker , glass rod , water bath , funnel ,etc

CHEMICALS : Formaldehyde (40%) , Urea , Conc. Sulphuric Acid , dist. water ,etc

Step 2: Preparation :

Audio explanation:

Place 5ml of (40%) Formaldeyhde solution in 100ml beaker. Add about 2.5 gram of urea while stirring until a saturated solution is formed

Step 3: Add Concentrated Sulphuric Acid

Audio explanation:

Add a few drops of Conc. Sulphuric Acid while stirring continuously during addition. Stir vigirously.

Step 4: Formation of the product

Audio explanation:

All of a sudden you will observe a voluminous white solid mass appear in the beaker. Collect this product in the filter paper over a funnel and let it dry.

Step 5: Collect the product

Audio explanation:

And after the product is dried , weigh it . Thus you have made Urea-Formaldehyde resin in your experiment.
